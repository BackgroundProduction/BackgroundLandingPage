# 3D Asset Licenses

Most 3D motifs (stage spotlights, laurel wreath, film reel, brass horn,
medals, podium, velvet curtains, red carpet) are **procedural Three.js
geometry** built in `components/canvas/motifs/` — no external files, no
licenses required.
